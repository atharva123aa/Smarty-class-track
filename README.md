Smarty-Class-Track🎓
"The class of yours deserve better, be smart and tracky"

#Built for teachers and students for smart interactions and trackings.
https://web-production-add06e.up.railway.app/

A flask and web based app basically frontend+backend.
It is a web app for-
1.Managing and creating student ids.
2.Marking their Attendance and viewing it.
3.Marking the test scores of student and viewing it.
4.Accesing the Reports os students and their report card
5.Interactive quiz creation with preview quizes and even get your score.
6.all sync with each other for better tracking.

#Tech stack
-Python
-Flask
-HTML
-CSS
-JS
